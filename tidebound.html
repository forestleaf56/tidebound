Survivor's Log

MOVE — left thumb joystick (or WASD). Drag right side to look (or mouse). ACT button jumps.

SURVIVE — watch hunger 🍖 and thirst 💧. Grill fish, purify seawater, farm crops on deck plots.

GATHER — tap ● at flotsam with your hook, chop palms with an axe, mine stone and ore in caves.

BUILD — craft deck tiles, sails, grills and purifiers, then select them in the hotbar and tap to place. Stand by a sail and press STEER to pilot your raft.

LEVEL UP — everything grants XP. New recipes unlock each level — and the ocean grows meaner. Sharks arrive at level 2.

ISLANDERS — castaways on islands offer quests: gathering, hunting, fishing, exploring. Return to them to claim rewards.
